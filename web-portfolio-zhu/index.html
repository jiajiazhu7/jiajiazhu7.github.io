About Studio

JAJA Record is an independent record store in OTTAWA. In 2018, we opened up first location in Nepean neighbourhood. We’re known for our excellent customer service, music knowledge, and eclectic selection of new & used LPs, CDs, turntables and accessories.

Special For You
